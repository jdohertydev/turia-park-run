What is Turia Park Run?

Turia Parkrun 5k is a free race event held the first Saturday of every month at 09:00 in Turia Park to bring the running community of Valencia together. Using the 5k route already marked out in Turia Park, we run, jog, walk 'downstream'. Runners keep their own times (if they want) and after, go for a well-earned breakfast.

Can I run with you?

Yes, of course! We want runners of all ages and abilities to join us. No matter where you think your running currently is, you'll always be welcomed with open arms. We even have volunteer coaches who can offer invaluable advice as well as training sessions.

Where do you meet?

  • When: the first Saturday of every month.
  • Starting point: Turia Park, Km 0.
  • Our route: Turia Parkun 5k - Downstream.
  • Where we get coffee afterward: Rito Tostadores.
